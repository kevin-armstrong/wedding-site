New York City

Transportation
Get to NYC via the Metro North. Train lines run direct from South Norwalk to Grand Central Terminal in NYC (approximately 60 minutes and $12 each way)
Restaurants
  • Calle Ocho (Latin American food, Upper West Side)
  • Wa Jeal (Chinese Sichuan food, Upper East Side)
  • Onieals Restaurant and Pub (Upscale Pub, SOHO)
  • Chelsea Market (Marketplace with great everything, Chelsea)
  • La Tarte Flambee (tiny place on Upper East Side, specializes in tartes flambees, super French)
  • Radegast Hall & Biergarten (Beer Garden, Williamsburg in Brooklyn)
Activites
  • The High Line A beautiful, linear park full of wonderful architecture, nearby to Chelsea Market
  • Museums...The Met, the MoMA, the Museum of Natural History and The Cloisters are a couple of our favorites
  • Broadway shows... check out the TKTS booth for discount same day tickets
