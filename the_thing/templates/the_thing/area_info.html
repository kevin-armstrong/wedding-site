Local :: Fairfield County, CT

Hotels
Courtyard by Marriott 474 Main Ave, Norwalk, CT 06851
Restaurants
  • Oak and Almond Upscale American, one of Kayla's favorites. It's super convenient if you're staying at the Courtyard in Norwalk.
  • Schoolhouse at Canondale We haven't actually been here yet because it's always booked for Brunch, but it looks really cute and has great reviews.
  • Valencia Luncheria Venzuelan beach food; arepas and empandas are great, though a bit on the fried side (which could be good or bad depending on your perspective).
  • The Ginger Man Bar with a huge selection of beers; even Kevin can find beers he likes.
  • In Thai Solid Thai food. Kevin gets the Crispy Chicken Basil at least once a week. Yet, Kayla still asks every time, "So what did you get?"
Activities
  • Norwalk Maritime Aquarium We had a corporate event here once where they catered sushi. Yes, awkward.
